About

Heroes of Olympus

Heroes of Olympus is a fantasy game taking place in a Greek Mythological inspired setting. Our game will follow five powerful Demigods as they attempt to save the world from an apocalypse unleashed by a Mad Titan. Heroes of Olympus is a Turn Based Tactical RPG with a focus on strategic gameplay.

Meet Your Heroes!

Hercules

Hercules, also called Heracles, is known as the greatest Greek hero, son of Zues and the mortal woman Alcmene. Hercules is famous for his godlike strength and his many adventures, including travelling to the Underworld twice.

Achilles

Achilles is the son of Thetis and Peleus. As a child, Achilles was dipped into the River Styx to make him immortal. His mother, Thetis, dipped Achilles by his heel meaning his heel did not touch the water and is the only part of his body that can be harmed.

Hippolyta

Hippolyta is the daughter of Ares, the god of war. Hippolyta was the queen of the Amazons, known to be the strongest and wisest of the Amazons.

Theseus

Theseus is the son of either Posiedon and Aethra. Upon reaching adulthood, Theseus journeyed out to Athens knowing it would be a dangerous adventure. Rather than taking the safer route of the sea, he instead chose to travel by land and came across many challenges along the way, such as bandits or a giant.

Helen

Helen was the daughter of Zues and Leda. Helen was known as the most beautiful woman of Greece.

What to Expect!

Heroes of Olympus is heavily rooted from Greek Mythology as it takes inspiration from classic characters and tales.

Team Spooki have drawn on these stories and created a game with its own unique twist. Combining infamous figures and strategic tactics to fight a common enemy is what will set Heroes of Olympus apart!

+ Featuring 5 Main Levels + Strategic and Turn-Based Application + 2D Tiled-Map with various obstacles + Five Main Heroes with different abilities + Increased difficulty in defeating Titans with each passing level
