The Hack

In total there were four tasks to complete for The Hack. A Quality Strategy Report, a Game Design Document, the homepage for our game website and a screencast displaying the first level of our game, Heroes Of Olympus. Although, we had a lot to do we enjoyed going further into the design process and putting our idea into practise!

It was fun to complete the game design document and really go in depth into what greek heroes we would like in our game along with our enemies of course! Also, it was great to begin coding our game. We managed to create a map, with heroes who were able to move and for a good measure created a team logo too!
