MVP

For the MVP our full focus was on working on our software until it was 'release ready'. This meant working together as a team and figuring out the most important aspects to our game! Along with this, we continued the development for our website. So the team was split into sub-teams, with each sub-team working on their own respective task.

Even though the work load was quite heavy (given the time of year) we managed to pull through and present our game to our peers! It was a daunting but an intriguing experience as it allowed us to view other games whilst observing the reaction to Heroes of Olympus!
